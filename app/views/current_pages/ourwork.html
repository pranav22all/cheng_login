Our Work

Here's some in-depth information about a few of our various projects here at the Cheng Foundation.

The CII Project

The CII (Cultural Immigration and Integration) Project is the newest Cheng Foundation venture, through which the organization strives to increase cultural awareness.

Join the CII Project

The Shaw Trust

The Shaw Trust is dedicated towards improving the lives of students around the world. Currently, the Trust is focused on funding the development of helicopter pads on top of local high schools.

Get involved with the Shaw Trust

The Chemboost Project

A collaboration with Cheng Foundation member Allen Zhang, in order to run the non-profit organization, Chemboost.net. The organization strives to boost millions of students' chemistry elo across the globe.

Visit Chemboost.net

The Madduri Partnership

A partnership with fellow billionaire Sathvik Madduri to find the rarest artificats in existence. The partnership is responsible for the discovery of the Rosetta Stone and a multitude of other ancient works.

Learn more about the partnership

The Cheng Grant Organization

An organization providing financial grants to low-income students (annual incomes less than $100 million) in our communities. Applications are reviewed by the Cheng Grant Committee and are very competitive.

Apply for a Cheng Grant
